Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your email address and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.

We do not collect payment information, precise location data, or any information beyond what is needed to provide the Service.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To authenticate you and keep your account secure.
  • To display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • To respond to support requests or questions you send us.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to providing the Service.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secured servers. Passwords are never stored in plain text. We take reasonable technical measures to protect your information from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except in the following limited circumstances:

  • When required by law or in response to a valid legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Spendly or its users.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by contacting us through the app. Upon deletion, your data will be permanently removed from our systems.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.

7. Contact

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please reach out to us through the app.
